Recommendations of the best video games (in my opinion)

Masterpiece

  • Alien Isolation

    Being a huge fan of the Alien universe and of H.R. Giger, this game is not only one of the best Horror Games on the market, it also respects the franchise wonderfully. Every aspect of this game is well done, from the art direction, to the soundscape, the tension in the gameplay and the storytelling.

    It's prodigious. If you're an Alien fan, don't wait, it's really impossible to be disappointed.

    VIEW IN STORE
  • Cyberpunk 2077

    A narrative and visual masterpiece. I've never been so captivated by a video game. The visual and audio atmosphere, the dialogues and the romances really grabbed me by the guts. For the first time, a video game succeeded in getting me sentimentally attached to characters.

    Once I'd finished the game, I felt a void and a lack, as I recalled certain memories from the story. I had the same impression with the Phantom Liberty DLC, just as exceptional in quality.

    Definitely an absolute must if you love Cyberpunk and deep storytelling.

    VIEW IN STORE
  • Hunt Showdown

    An extraction shooter made by Crytek that mixes Wild West, Bayou, Zombies and Monsters that works too damn well, the atmosphere is extremely well crafted and oppressive, right down to the little noises that can betray you.

    Play it in the dark, preferably with a headset, it's an online game but it's really worth it, the Art Direction is exceptional and unique.

    VIEW IN STORE
  • Project Zomboid

    Simply the Best Zombie game ever made, there's no debate.

    Don't be fooled by its old-school look, I say with full confidence that this is the most realistic game of its genre.

    A Masterpiece not in its visuals, but in its mechanics and overall atmosphere.

    VIEW IN STORE

A Memorable Experience

  • Stray

    A little cat game without too much dialogue, but which manages to tell a touching story. Its strength also lies in the sumptuous Art Direction, and this world that mixes cats and robots, it's really a unique game that I won't forget.

  • Detroit : Become Human

    Would you be open to considering androids as beings with equal rights if they became as similar and more human than humans ?

A Great Journey

  • The Invincible

    Based on the novel by Stanisław Lem, it takes place on an unknown planet with Soviet-style equipment.

    Captivating game, unique Art Direction. I had a great time on this walking simulator, living the story of a scientist cosmonaut who finds herself lost in a mystery on an unknown planet, hunted by an anomaly of unknown origin.

    VIEW IN STORE
  • The Callisto Protocol

    Taking inspiration from several other notable pieces of science fiction, it still found a place in my heart. It's a very well developed game, and the narrative is immersive.

    The journey was really great.

    VIEW IN STORE

Worth The Time

  • The Finals

    A Fast FPS made by Embark studio (Ex-Battlefield Guys) that is absolutely revolutionary in its conception.

    I play it very regularly, and it deserves its place among the best fast fps of all time. May The Finals live forever!
