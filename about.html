About Me (Luke Aegis)

Hello ! Initially aspiring professional Concept-Artist/Illustrator, I'm Passionate about visual creation, whether through digital art, video games or screenshots. This site is a portfolio of my personal work, but also a gallery for sharing my in-game screenshots and some recommendations on the subject.

I couldn't find any reliable mainstream sites to invest myself in sharing screenshots online, so I created my own site because screenshots are made to be seen, it's useful to get references too, and this was also an opportunity to create my own sovereignty on the Portfolio side. If ArtStation becomes even more an AI-Land or disappears, this site will still be there, and will evolve.

I'm very interested in high-impact atmospheres and art directions. You'll notice that I'm a big fan of Science Fiction.

I don't claim to be the best artist on earth, and I only do it as a hobby from now on, although I'm open to opportunities, my aim is to share my passion, and enjoy what I do. Hope you like it !
