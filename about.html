About me (Luke Aegis)

Hello ! Passionate about visual creation, whether through digital art, video games or screenshots. This site is a portfolio of my personal work, but also a gallery for sharing my in-game screenshots and some recommendations on the subject.

I'm very interested in high-impact atmospheres and art directions. You'll notice that I'm a big fan of Science Fiction.

I don't claim to be the best artist on the planet, and I only do it as a hobby from now on, although I'm open to opportunities, my aim is to share my passion as much as possible, and enjoy what I do.
